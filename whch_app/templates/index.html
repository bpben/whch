About

Source

Data comes from the GDELT project, a database of what's happening around the world.

Reading

We read in stories being reported from five major networks (Fox, Associated Press, Reuters, Washington Post and Huffington Post.

Learning

Our model learns relationships from this data and creates a model to predict whether a story will be reported.

Predicting

Based on your input, the model can predict how likely each of the major networks is to report on the subject you care about.
